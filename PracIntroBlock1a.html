Study Introduction

To begin we want to introduce you to a speeded categorization task that we will return to later.

GO/NO-GO Task

In this GO/NO-GO task, you will be shown a series of words in the center of the computer screen. Some of the words will belong to TARGET CATEGORIES. If you see a word that belongs to a target category, your task is to “GO” by pressing the SPACEBAR as quickly as possible. In contrast, if you see a word that does NOT belong to a target category, you should simply ignore it (“NO-GO”).

  • For example, if the target category is WHITE OBJECTS you should press the SPACE BAR as fast as you can every time you see a white-object word, and ignore all other words.
  • Each word will only remain on the screen for a fraction of a second.
  • You need to make a response before the word disappears and if you fail to, this will be counted as an error and a red X will appear.
  • If you press the SPACE BAR when the word is NOT a target word (not a BIRD), this will also be an error and two blue exclamation marks !! will appear.
  • The task is very fast and you will make mistakes! That’s okay, just pick up and go on to the next trial. 

Press the SPACEBAR to continue.
